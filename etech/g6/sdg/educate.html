Always be aware of your surroundings. Know how to escape if necessary. Make sure your mobile phone is with you, that it is charged, and that it is topped off with enough credit. Be aware of your surroundings. Know how to make an assistance call. Make sure your phone has important contact information, such as your parents' phone number and perhaps a cab company. And trust your instincts. This might be your best opportunity to escape a dangerous situation or a way to get help. Your senses and intuition.
